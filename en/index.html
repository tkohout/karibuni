Restaurant

In Swahili, "Karibu" means you are welcome, and is often used to invite people to share food. Whenever I think of my time in Africa, the word Karibu brings back memories of sharing and community.

Walking down the street and having friendly locals calling out "Karibu" to invite the sharing of their meal. Karibuni is the plural of this - you are all welcome - and this is the soul of Karibuni Tapas.

Flavours of the world

I was backpacking around the world for almost 7 years, across 6 of the 7 continents. During this time I experienced people and cultures that I had never known of back home in England, and I also fell in love with the food I ate on my journey. I learnt how to cook the dishes how they are made traditionally and have now brought these recipes to Valencia to share the food I love through the restaurant.

- Abby Payne, The Cook
